Miro is a commercial forestry and timber products group operating in West Africa. We operate in Ghana and Sierra Leone and are focused on the production of plywood for the construction market (both regional and overseas), transmission poles for electrification and sawn timber panel products. Currently, we operate over 16,000ha of sustainable plantations we have established ourselves with a team of approximately 2,000 people. We are expanding at a rate of 1,500 to 3,000ha per annum.
